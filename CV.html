Professional Summary

Passionate multilingual professional with a unique career path shaped by a deep curiosity for “otherness” — the understanding and appreciation of different cultures, perspectives, and experiences. Today, I bring together my expertise in SaaS, UX design, multilingual communication, and web design with no-code templates to create products and experiences that truly resonate with diverse audiences.
